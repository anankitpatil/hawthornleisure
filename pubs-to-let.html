Pubs to Let

 

Hawthorn Leisure was formed in 2013 to acquire, invest in and support pubs across the United Kingdom.

Our strategy is to work in close partnership with our tenants, providing management support and capital investment to help them drive growth and maximise the potential of their pubs.

 

Hawthorn Leisure owns over 360 public houses across the UK, and operates a wide variety of

 

 

different types of pubs: from local / traditional community pubs to country inns and high-street pubs.

 

Our pubs are primarily operated by licensed tenants who benefit from extensive support from Hawthorn in the form of management focus, capital investment, extensive product range, training and infrastructure.
